Biography

Don Norman is a pioneer in the field of Human–Computer Interaction. He is the director of The Design Lab at the University of California, San Diego. He is best known for his books on design, especially "The Design of Everyday Things."

Core Design Principles
  • Affordances: Physical properties that suggest how to use an object.
  • Signifiers: Visual cues that tell you where to interact.
  • Feedback: Clear communication about the result of an action.

The Father of UX

"Good design is actually a lot harder to notice than poor design, in part because good designs fit our needs so well that the design is invisible."
